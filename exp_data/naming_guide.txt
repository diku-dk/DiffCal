The first position corresponds to deformation mode i.e.
h: hanging
t: twisting
o: oscillating

The second position corresponds to material type i.e.
e: Ecoflex-50
m: MoldStar-15

The third position corresponds to shape i.e.
c: cantilever
s: spine
d: dragon
f: finger

Example:
t_mem_c -> twisting_moldstar+ecoflex+moldstar_cantilever
